## Note YAML Frontmatter Format

```yaml
---
title: "<Topic Name>"
subject: "<Subject>"
type: concept-note | worksheet | ai-output | explanation
date: YYYY-MM-DD
tags: [<subject-slug>, <topic-slug>]
source: manual | claude | gemini | ollama
reviewed: false
---
```

- Set `reviewed: true` once you have verified AI content against a trusted source
- `type` drives Dataview queries — keep it consistent
